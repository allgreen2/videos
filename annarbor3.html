Feldenkrais Institute of Ann Arbor

Training Schedule


Online Instruction


Students receive both online and in-person training. Online classes are live and generally held on the weekends so working students can attend.


In-Person Training Retreats


In-person training retreats are 2 weeks long and held twice a year at the beginning and end of the summer. Functional Integration are provided to each student during the live segment and 12 personalized lessons are part of the live segment. Luggage storage is available if needed on days when you are arriving and departing.

Feldenkrais Institute of Ann Arbor

Tuition Information

Investment


Students pay $1950 three times a year for 40 days of instruction. The program offers a total of 160 days of instruction and 12 Functional Integration lessons provided by training staff. Students can take advantage of bonus material to deepen their training or make up missed days at no additional cost. A deposit of $1950 is required to hold your spot in the program. This deposit is not refundable and goes towards your last tuition payment at the end of the 4th year.

Late Payments


If your payment is late, a fee may be charged to restore your status in the program. Your tuition payments must be current in order to attend training.
